Battery Pack Modeling

Brief Description

The plant is made up of:
  • battery pack
  • cell monitoring unit
  • pre-charge circuit
  • charger and load

Battery pack

The battery pack is made up of several modules in series. Each module is made up of several cells in series.

Pre-charge circuit

The pre-charge circuit is made up of several switching devices or contactors (like Relays), resistors and capacitors that, when appropriately controlled, aim at making the voltage of the load/charger equal to that of the battery so as to avoid current spikes.

Charger and load

In the model, charger and load are represented by a source of positive and negative current, respectively. This simplifies what could be instead represented by an electric generator or an electric motor, for instance.

Model

Open Battery Pack Model
This model includes two variants of Battery Pack (1. One module with 6 cells in series, 2. 16 modules with 96 cells in total). Battery Pack is connected to Charger / Load via Precharge Circuit and contactors. Precharge circuit is required ensure there is no sudden rush of current in/out of battery pack when connected to Charger or Load with large voltage difference. Closing or opening precharge contactors is done by BMS.
